About Me

Hi, my name is Hoang Y Vy Nguyen (Y Vy). I'm a Multimedia Designer & Developer based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. I’m all about bold, eye-catching visuals and have a huge passion for design. I love bringing ideas to life through detailed and powerful designs that grab attention, share a message, and make people feel something. Whether it’s a sleek brand, cool packaging, digital media, or merch, I mix creativity and precision to make your vision stand out. Let’s team up and turn your idea into something unforgettable—where great design makes a real impact!
